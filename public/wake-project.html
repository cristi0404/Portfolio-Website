Wake (in development)

Wake is a passion project in the form of a short, 2D interactive adventure about the fear of loss. When the COVID-19 pandemic first started, I found myself with a lot of unexpected time and unprocessed feelings, so I decided to try my hand at game design, something that has always been in the back of my mind. I wrote the story throughout 2020, made a Twine game out of it, then decided I wanted to create a more developed version with original art and music and a focus on meaningful mechanics. Wake is currently in development and is expected to release as a free-to-play browser game during Summer 2021.
